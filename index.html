Health Insurance Risk Calculator

Answer the following questions and find out where you are placed based on the health insurane risk. This risk assessment will be determined by age, body-mass index, blood pressure, and family history of disease.

Normal BMI ranges from 18.5-24.9. Overweight BMI ranges from 25-29.9. Obese BMI ranges from 30-34.9. For normal blood pressure, it should be less than 120 over less than 80. For elevated blood pressure the ranges should be 120-129 over less than 80. For Stage 1 blood pressure, it should range 130-139 over 80-89. For stage 2 blood pressure, it should range 140 or higher over 90 or higher. For crisis blood pressure, it should range higher than 180 over higher than 120.
